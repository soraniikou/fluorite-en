 fluorite 💎

 
> **"Two feelings. One light. Crystallize your conflicting emotions into a poetic truth."**

`fluorite` is a minimalist emotional sanctuary and mindfulness app designed to hold your "Light" (wishes) and "Shadow" (fears) without judgment. Using AI, it transforms your internal conflicts into a single, beautiful "Crystal" of poetic wisdom.



 ✨ Concept: Live Lighter
In our daily lives, we often carry contradictory feelings—the intense spark of a new love and the quiet fear of losing it; the desire to change and the comfort of staying the same. 

`fluorite` provides a ritual to:
1. **Acknowledge** both sides of your heart.
2. **Disintegrate** the weight of those thoughts.
3. **Crystallize** them into a gentle message that guides you forward.

---

 🎨 Features

 ✦ Crystallize
Input your **Light** (what you are reaching toward) and your **Shadow** (what is holding you back). Watch as your words physically disintegrate and transform into a unique poetic "Crystal" generated by AI (Heart Fluorite).

🫙 Time Capsule (Seal a Moment)
Not ready to crystallize? Seal your current state in a digital bottle.
- **Text & Voice:** Record your raw emotions via microphone or write them down.
- **Atmospheric Recording:** A dedicated space to whisper your hopes or sighs.

📦 Past Bottles (Archive)
A collection of your past moments. Revisit your journey by tapping on previous bottles to see how your heart has evolved over time.

 🌈 Three Soul Hues
Switch between three atmospheric themes to match your current mood:
- 💙 **Blue Flame:** For deep reflection and clarity.
- 💚 **Green Mist:** For healing and tranquility.
- 💜 **Purple Dream:** For creative and spiritual connection.

---

Technical Stack
- **Frontend:** React.js
- **Styling:** CSS-in-JS (Custom Minimalist UI)
- **AI Engine:** Anthropic Claude API (Sonnet)
- **Visuals:** Custom Particle Disintegration System (CSS Transitions)
- **Storage:** LocalStorage (Privacy-first: your data stays on your device)
